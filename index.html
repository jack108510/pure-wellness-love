I come from a lineage of healing women. My greatest lessons have been learning to honor our yin and feminine gifts — to forgive, to embrace the whole family, and to love unconditionally. Our 5 pillars are designed to bring you expert guidance for a wholistic well being approach. Flowers can't be forced to bloom… our approach is delicate, whole family, and honoring.

Relationships

Spirit

Living in alignment with love, nature, and divine rhythm

Our logo carries two symbols: the Sampaguita — the Jasmine flower, national flower of the Philippines, meaning 'sumpa kita,' a promise of love. And the Sand Dollar — which, when broken open, releases five peace doves. Five pillars. Five doves. One promise of love.

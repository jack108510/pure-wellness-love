About Dr. Jasmine

Mrs. Maui · Author · Founder of The Living Love Collective

Dr. Jasmine Therese Esguerra is a Hawaiian-based healer, author, and spiritual guide whose work bridges ancestral Filipino wisdom with modern wellness practice. As Mrs. Maui, she embodies the spirit of aloha — love, compassion, and deep connection to land and community.

Through her Living Love philosophy, she has guided thousands toward sacred embodiment, environmental purity, and generational healing. Her Sampaguita and Bayani pillars form the foundation of a practice that honors both the divine feminine and the courage of community.

Her book Divine Spark and her curated product lines bring the essence of Hawaiian botanicals and Filipino sacred traditions into homes around the world.


The Living Love Philosophy

Two sacred pillars. One transformative path.

Sampaguita

The sacred jasmine of the Philippines — symbol of purity, devotion, and the divine feminine. Sampaguita teaches us that true strength is found in gentleness, that the most powerful fragrance comes from the smallest blossom, and that environmental purity is the foundation of spiritual wellness.

Bayani

The spirit of communal courage — the Filipino tradition of heroic service to others. Bayani reminds us that we heal together, that our ancestors walk beside us, and that the highest expression of love is showing up for our families and communities with unwavering honor.

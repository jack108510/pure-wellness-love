The Living Love Philosophy

"Living LOVE is the healing technique I've developed from understanding and working with our vibrational being."

At the heart of Pure Wellness Joy is a sacred methodology — a bridge between ancient Filipino healing traditions and modern vibrational science. Two pillars uphold this work:

Sampaguita

The Feminine Principle

Like the jasmine flower — delicate yet enduring — the feminine principle embodies receptivity, intuition, and the sacred art of allowing healing to flow through surrender and grace.

Bayani

The Masculine Principle

Drawn from the Filipino spirit of communal courage — bayanihan — the masculine principle embodies protector energy, purposeful action, and the strength that comes from serving something greater.

Divine Spark

"Every Family Carries a Spark of Healing"

A forthcoming book that reveals how every family lineage carries a divine spark — a unique frequency of healing waiting to be remembered, activated, and lived. Dr. Jasmine weaves personal memoir, ancestral wisdom, and practical vibrational tools into a guide for families ready to reclaim their wholeness.

"When we heal, we don't heal alone. The ripples move backward through our lineage and forward through our children. This is not metaphor — it is vibrational truth. Every family carries a spark. Your job is not to create it, but to remember it."
